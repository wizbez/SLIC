Games

Here are some calming games to help reduce stress and to keep you entertained.

Snake.

The oldschool favorite called "Snake" where you are small at first and try to collect food to make you bigger, but try to not bump your head into the wall or your own body. (Ouch)

Tetris.

Another oldschool favorite called "Tetris" where you put different shapes in place and reshape your mind (Not literally.)

Racing.

A cool racing game to reduce stress and let your mind be on the road.

2048.

2048 is sort of a math game were you mix up a number with other numbers and try to get to the number "2048."

Red driver.

An awesome racing game with perfect quality and stress free! (DO NOT go to a different game on the same website.)

Solitare.

A card game that never gets old which you have to get the cards in a row. A good break for your mind.

Sonic Ultimate Flash.

Have fun with the awesome speedy character sonic and his friends and complete every mission to keep your mind off stress and into his speed.
